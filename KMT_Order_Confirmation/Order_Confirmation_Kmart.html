CUSTOMER RESPONSIBILITIES
• A responsible adult, 18 years or older, must be present during delivery.
• In inclement weather, please provide a clear path — no ice, snow or debris — from the street to the appropriate entrance.
• Confine all house pets to an area separate from the delivery path.
• If merchandise is to be left on the porch, in the garage or outdoors, a responsible neighbor or representative must accompany the Sears team and sign for the delivery.
DELIVERY TEAM RESPONSIBILITIES
Our delivery experts will...
• Remove or relocate to an accessible location on the delivery premises one like item for each new item delivered.
• Connect delivered appliances to existing home utilities per "Product-Specific Guidelines," using connector lines supplied by the manufacturer or by Sears.
• Remove packing materials from the delivery premises.
• Install an anti-tip device for free-standing and slide-in range deliveries with hookup.
• Determine and offer for sale any needed parts/accessories or installation services — not included in the appliance purchase — to safely connect appliances to home utilities.
• Use your new, supplied parts or accessories that meet manufacturer requirements and "Product-Specific Delivery Guidelines."
Our delivery experts will not...
• Use customer-supplied or used connectors.
• Remove their shoes, though caution and care will be exercised to protect your home and merchandise.
• Lift/hoist merchandise over banisters or other obstructions or through windows to complete delivery.
• Relocate used appliances to a different address.
• Perform carpentry, electrical or plumbing work.
• Deliver and hook up a free-standing or slide-in range without installing a range anti-tip device.
• Install appliance trim kits.
